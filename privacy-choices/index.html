Last updated: April 8, 2026

This page explains how to manage website analytics preferences and how to make privacy, access, correction, or deletion requests related to YouOweMe.

Manage website analytics

You can choose whether this browser allows optional website analytics. This setting affects the current browser and device. If you clear site data or use another browser or device, you may be asked again.

Website analytics preference: not set.

How to request deletion or privacy help

  • If you use YouOweMe only locally and do not use optional sync or sharing features, you can usually remove local data directly in the app or by removing the app from your device.
  • If your app version provides in-app account management or deletion controls, you should use those controls where available for the fastest result.
  • If you use optional account, sync, cloud-profile, support, or sharing features and want to request deletion, access, or correction of information we control, email eugen_yablonski@yahoo.com.
  • Please include enough information for us to identify the relevant account, communication, or shared output. We may need to verify your identity before completing the request.

Suggested email subject: Privacy Request

Your rights

Depending on your location and the applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection. You may also be able to withdraw consent for optional processing where consent is the legal basis.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with similar rights, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
