What is You Owe Me?

You Owe Me is an iPhone app for tracking money between people. It helps with IOUs, shared bills, temporary support, informal loans, repayments, recurring costs, reminders, Live Links, PDF statements, and follow-up messages — so you can see who owes whom, what changed, and what to say next.

Use it when the amount does not stay simple: someone repays part, another cost is added, a due date changes, a recurring bill renews, or you need to share the balance without forcing the other person into the app.

How YouOweMe keeps shared money clear

1

Log what happened

Add money lent, money borrowed, bills paid, shared expenses, recurring costs, or repayments.

2

See the balance and timeline

Every entry updates the balance. Timeline keeps the money story with that person organized beyond a simple transaction list.

3

Follow up or share clearly

Check what already happened, then set a reminder for a due date, bill, balance, or follow-up, generate a calmer money message, send a Live Link, or create a PDF statement when the situation needs more clarity.

FAQ

What is You Owe Me?

You Owe Me is an iPhone app for tracking money between people. It helps with IOUs, shared bills, temporary support, informal loans, repayments, recurring costs, reminders, Live Links, PDF statements, and follow-up messages.

Is YouOweMe only for loans?

No. It can track informal loans, IOUs, shared expenses, temporary support, family reimbursements, roommate bills, partner spending, client balances, recurring costs, and repayments over time.

Can it help me ask someone to pay me back politely?

Yes. Money Conversations can generate a follow-up message from the real balance and history, with a tone that fits the relationship.

Does the other person need the app?

Not always. With Live Link, one person can manage the balance in YouOweMe while the other person opens a browser link to see the latest statement and transaction history.

Can I use it for family reimbursements?

Yes. You can track purchases, bills, subscriptions, recurring charges, and repayments for parents, siblings, children, or other family members. If the main job is paying for an elderly parent, start with the elderly parent expense tracker.

Can I split bills and shared expenses?

Yes. You can split expenses equally or with custom amounts, then keep the result in a running balance instead of recalculating every time.

Does it work offline?

Yes. YouOweMe can be used without mandatory sign-up and supports offline tracking.
